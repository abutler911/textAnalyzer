Reading Level Reference Table

Flesch-Kincaid Grade Level Education Level Description
0 - 1 Kindergarten Very easy to read. Basic words and sentence structures.
2 - 3 1st to 2nd Grade Easy to read. Simple words and sentences.
4 - 5 3rd to 4th Grade Fairly easy to read.
6 - 7 5th to 6th Grade Plain English. Easily understandable by 11 to 13-year-olds.
8 - 9 7th to 8th Grade Fairly difficult to read.
10 - 12 High School Challenging for the average reader. Suitable for ages 15-17.
13 - 15 College More difficult to read. Best understood by university graduates.
16 and above College Graduate Very difficult to read. Best understood by advanced degree holders.
